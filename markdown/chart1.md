# Soil Grades
- Soil Grade 1
  - Basic soil quality
  - Normal crop growth
- Soil Grade 2
  - Improved soil quality
  - Faster crop growth
- Soil Grade 3
  - High soil quality
  - Even faster crop growth
- Soil Grade 4
  - Superior soil quality
  - Optimal crop growth
- Soil Grade 5
  - Best soil quality
  - Maximum crop growth
